💧 Water Quality Monitoring and Forecasting System

The Water Quality Monitoring and Forecasting System is a web-based application that monitors water quality parameters and predicts future water conditions using data analysis.
This system helps in identifying whether water is safe for consumption or polluted, supporting environmental monitoring and decision making.

🚀 Features

💧 Monitor water quality parameters

📊 Display water quality data in an easy-to-understand format

🔍 Analyze parameters like pH, turbidity, temperature, and dissolved oxygen

📈 Forecast future water quality conditions

⚠️ Identify whether water is safe or unsafe

📱 Simple and user-friendly interface
